Projects

ShopEase – E-Commerce Platform

ShopEase is a modern and scalable e-commerce platform developed to provide businesses with a complete online selling solution. The application offers a seamless shopping experience through an intuitive user interface, advanced product search, secure payment gateway integration, and responsive design across desktop and mobile devices. Customers can browse products, add items to their cart, manage wishlists, place orders, and track deliveries in real time. The administrative panel enables store owners to manage inventory, categories, promotions, customer orders, and sales reports efficiently. The platform was designed with performance, security, and scalability in mind, making it suitable for startups as well as growing enterprises looking to expand their digital presence.

EduSmart – School Management System

EduSmart is a comprehensive school management solution built to simplify academic and administrative operations for educational institutions. The system provides dedicated portals for administrators, teachers, students, and parents, ensuring seamless communication and efficient management of daily activities. It includes modules for student admissions, attendance tracking, fee management, examination scheduling, result publishing, timetable generation, and staff administration. Parents can monitor their children's academic progress and attendance online, while teachers can upload assignments and manage classroom records digitally. By automating manual processes and centralizing data, EduSmart enhances productivity and improves the overall educational experience.

MediCare HMS – Hospital Management System

MediCare HMS is an integrated healthcare management platform designed to streamline hospital operations and improve patient care. The system digitizes patient registration, appointment scheduling, medical records, billing, laboratory reports, and pharmacy management through a centralized dashboard. Doctors can access patient histories instantly, while administrative staff can efficiently manage appointments, invoices, and insurance details. The platform supports role-based access control to ensure data privacy and security, making it compliant with modern healthcare management standards. Its intuitive interface and automation capabilities help reduce paperwork, improve workflow efficiency, and provide a better experience for both healthcare providers and patients.

PropertyHub – Real Estate Portal

PropertyHub is a feature-rich real estate platform that connects buyers, sellers, property owners, and agents through a user-friendly digital marketplace. The application allows users to browse residential and commercial properties using advanced search filters based on location, budget, property type, and amenities. Interactive maps, high-quality image galleries, virtual property showcases, and inquiry forms provide an engaging user experience. Property owners and agents can manage listings through a dedicated dashboard, while administrators oversee approvals, featured properties, and user management. The platform is optimized for search engines and mobile devices, making it an effective solution for expanding online real estate businesses.

WorkForce Pro – HR & Payroll Management System

WorkForce Pro is an enterprise-grade human resource and payroll management system developed to simplify employee administration and workforce management. The platform centralizes employee records, attendance tracking, leave requests, payroll processing, and performance evaluations within a secure cloud-based environment. HR teams can automate salary calculations, tax deductions, and monthly payslip generation while managers can monitor employee productivity through detailed analytics and reporting tools. The system includes role-based permissions, document management, and workflow automation to improve operational efficiency and reduce manual errors. Designed for organizations of all sizes, WorkForce Pro enables businesses to manage their workforce with greater accuracy, transparency, and productivity.
