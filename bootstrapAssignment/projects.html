Project Overview

My journey in software development has provided me with opportunities to work on both Salesforce CRM solutions and Full-Stack Web Applications, where I have contributed to designing, developing, customizing, and maintaining business applications. Throughout this journey, I have collaborated with stakeholders to understand business requirements, translate them into technical solutions, and deliver applications that improve productivity, automate business processes, and enhance user experience.

In Salesforce development, I have worked with Lightning Web Components (LWC), Apex Classes, Apex Triggers, Salesforce Flows, SOQL, Custom Objects, Validation Rules, Reports & Dashboards, and other platform features to build scalable CRM solutions. My work includes analyzing business requirements, developing new functionalities, automating workflows, performing testing, deploying enhancements, and providing application support to ensure reliable and efficient business operations.

Alongside Salesforce, I have developed responsive full-stack web applications using HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MySQL. My responsibilities include designing user interfaces, building RESTful APIs, integrating databases, implementing authentication and authorization, performing CRUD operations, validating user inputs, and optimizing application performance to deliver secure and user-friendly applications.

Throughout the development process, I follow industry best practices including requirement analysis, application design, clean coding standards, debugging, testing, version control using Git, and Agile development methodologies. I am passionate about learning new technologies, solving business challenges, and building scalable software solutions that deliver meaningful value to organizations.
